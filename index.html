Building Trust Through Transparent AI

A UX Case Study on designing a trustworthy AI-powered healthcare triage assistant for the citizens of Quebec.

Understanding the Problem

There exists a big demand in Quebec for access to quality healthcare services. Key entrance services like preliminary health assessments and clear communication are critical, yet difficult to standardize. While Artificial Intelligence (AI) presents a promising solution, especially with user familiarity with chatbots, it faces unique challenges in healthcare: building trust, ensuring safety, and maintaining transparency.

HealthGuide QC is an AI-powered chatbot designed to be a robust, trustworthy entrance service for patients in the province. It aims to build trust through verified medical information, ensure safety by securely handling data, and promote transparency by explaining its recommendations clearly.

Research & Competitive Analysis

To produce a valuable solution, it was fundamental to understand Quebec’s healthcare challenges (long waiting lists, high job vacancy rates) and the existing market for AI-powered health assistants. Our research confirmed that while AI chatbots can improve accessibility and efficiency, they must be designed to overcome challenges of privacy, safety, and a perceived lack of empathy.

Key Competitor Insights

  • TELUS Health MyCare: Highlights the need for bilingual support, secure data handling, and clear pathways to human doctors.
  • Ada Health & Buoy Health: Show the value of broad symptom analysis and providing additional educational content.
  • Sensely’s Molly: Demonstrates the importance of a triage system to detect emergencies.

The Solution: Final Requirements

Based on our comprehensive research and user-centered design process, we defined a core set of requirements to ensure HealthGuide QC is trustworthy, accessible, and effective for all Quebecers.

  • Multilingual: Supports conversations in multiple languages.
  • Clarity: Delivers medical information for non-technical patients.
  • Availability: Provides 24/7 access to health guidance.
  • Assessment: Guides users through symptom checks and provides early insights.
  • Security: Implements strong privacy measures according to Canadian regulations.
  • Usability: Features an intuitive and accessible chatbot design.
  • Trust: Provides reliable medical guidance with evidence to build confidence.
  • Next Steps: Directs users to the appropriate health service.
